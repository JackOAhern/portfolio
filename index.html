About Me

Goals for this course

I am interested in UX/UI job positions, so I want to have web

development knowledge to complinment my work as a designer.

At the same time, you never really know where your future may lead,

and web-development could end up being a career for me. I love

the idea of designing websites and hope we do some cool projects

this semester. I know that coding is all about practice, so my goal

is to code a little bit each day during the week. I have friends who

are into coding for fun nowadays, and would love to share that passion.

Web development is by far the most enticing area of coding to me.
